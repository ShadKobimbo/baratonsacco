Our principal activity is to mobilize and manage member savings prudently and ensure they gain returns on their investment.

The history of Baraton Sacco is a story of real business transformation. It is the story of a sacco that has grown and prospered through embracing competitive, customer focused and innovative tenacity that has enabled it to compete and excel in service delivery.
